Are you passionate about Robotics and Artificial intelligence? Do you want to help shape the next generation of innovators? The Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Innovator Network is a vibrant community that promotes AI and robotics enthusiasts in advancing their skills through a collaborative peer-to-peer learning environment. We're excited to announce the launch of our volunteer-based mentorship program and invite experienced professionals to apply as mentors.

Why Become a Mentor?

As a mentor, you will play a pivotal role in guiding and inspiring aspiring AI/Robotics professionals. You will:

Knowledge Impact

Share your knowledge and experience with motivated mentees.

Assisting Mentees

Assist mentees in building their skills and confidence.

Collaboration

Foster a collaborative learning environment.

Hackaton Challenge

Participate in the final hackathon, where mentees showcase their projects.

Community Contribution

Contribute to a community that values innovation, creativity, and teamwork.

Who We Are Looking For?

We welcome mentors with a background in AI/Robotics, excellent communication skills, and a collaborative spirit. Ideal candidates will have:

  • At least 2-3 years of experience in AI/Robotics.
  • A passion for mentoring.
  • A commitment to regular sessions, both online and offline
  • A willingness to foster innovation and teamwork.
  • An interest in supporting a diverse and inclusive community.

How to Apply

If you are interested in becoming a mentor, we encourage you to apply! The application process is simple:

  • Fill out our online application form (https://forms.gle/vat2Aiuyv2Gj6SAEA)
  • Provide details about your experience in AI/Robotics and your approach to mentoring.
  • Share your availability for regular sessions

Join Us!

By joining our mentorship program, you'll be part of a dynamic community that thrives on collaboration and innovation. If you are ready to make a difference and guide the next generation of AI/Robotics professionals, we look forward to hearing from you!
