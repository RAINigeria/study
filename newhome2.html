About

Research. Training. Innovation.

RAIN is a world-class research and training facility for robotics and AI, operating from Ibadan, Nigeria. With an average of 30 trainees per cohort and a team of highly skilled developers and academics, RAIN delivers globally competitive education.

Foreign diplomats — including the Deputy Secretary General of the United Nations, the Canadian Trade Commissioner, and a UNDP delegation — have visited RAIN's facility. Graduates are working across the globe.

Ibadan, Nigeria ~30 trainees/cohort Global Alumni UN Partners MIT LaunchX 2022

FAQ

How much does it cost?
Full 12-month programs are ₦7,700,000 each. Short courses are ₦3,000,000. Instalment payments may be available.
Is accommodation available?
Yes. RAIN provides en-suite on-campus rooms for ₦1,700,000/year including internet, AC, 24/7 power, and security.
Do I need prior experience?
No. Our courses take you from zero to hero in Python, computer vision, ML, and neural networks. All backgrounds welcome.
Can international students apply?
Yes. RAIN has a dedicated student support service to guide your study visa application to Nigeria.
