About

Research. Training. Innovation.

RAIN is a world-class research and training facility for robotics and AI, operating from Ibadan, Nigeria. With an average of 30 trainees per cohort and a team of highly skilled developers and academics, RAIN delivers globally competitive education.

Foreign diplomats — including the Deputy Secretary General of the United Nations, the Canadian Trade Commissioner, and a UNDP delegation — have visited RAIN's facility. Graduates are working across the globe.

FAQ

How much does it cost?
The full 12-month programs are ₦7,700,000 each. Short courses are ₦3,000,000. Instalment payments may be allowed. Check directly for the latest discounts and payment plans.
Is accommodation available?
Yes. RAIN provides comfortable on-campus en-suite rooms for ₦1,700,000/year, including internet, AC, power, water heater, and security — within the RAIN environment.
Do I need prior experience?
No. Our certification courses take you from zero to hero in Python, computer vision, ML, and neural networks. All backgrounds are welcome.
Can international students apply?
Absolutely. RAIN welcomes international students and has a dedicated student support service to guide your study visa application to Nigeria.
