Academic Partnership

Train at RAIN. Walk into Year 3 of a B.Sc. Degree.

RAIN has partnered with a fully NUC-accredited open university in Nigeria. Complete your RAIN programme, earn up to 24 university transfer credits, and skip straight to Year 3 — no JAMB required.

🎓
The RAIN Degree Pathway
RAIN training (1–2 years) → up to 24 NUC university credits → skip Years 1 & 2 → complete Year 3 & 4 of a B.Sc. in Data Science, Software Engineering, or IT → graduate with RAIN certification, Meta AI certification, and an accredited degree.

By 2027, the degree will be directly recognised in the USA — no foreign credential evaluation (WES) needed. Total cost: approximately ₦11.9M for world-class technical training plus a fully accredited B.Sc.
